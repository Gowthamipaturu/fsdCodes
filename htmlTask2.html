How to design a favicon using favicon.cc

Following are the steps to cerate a favicon:

  • Open google.com
  • Go to favicon.cc website
  • To create a favicon firstly, choose a color as per your requirement
  • Click on the squares and paint your logo
  • Download when you are finished
  • Put the favicon.ico file into your directory
  • Link the favicon in the code using relative path.
